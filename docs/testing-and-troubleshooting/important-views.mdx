title: Important Views
---

Following views are most used and are important to get details.

1. **CORPORATE_VIEW**: A source for WPS corporates AND Individuals (not employees) with all their details.
2. **CORPORATE_EMPLOYEE_VIEW**: A source for employees of WPS corporations and individuals
3. **SIF_FUND_MAPPING_VIEW**: A view used to identify whcih SIF is eligible for which funding.

- E.g A sif without any funding is eligible for SIF CREATION/FAT/SIF funding.
- A SIF with only SIF Creation funding is eligible for both SIF/FAT funding
- A SIF with SIF funding is not available for SIF Creation/FAT funding
- In short this view gives search result of sifs for funding.
